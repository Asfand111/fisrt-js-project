Important Things

AL-QURAN

The Quran is the holy book of Islam, serving as a guiding light for millions of Muslims around the world, offering wisdom, spiritual guidance, and a path to righteousness. Its teachings and revelations are considered the literal word of God, making it a source of inspiration, comfort, and strength for those who seek to live a virtuous life.

Al-Hadees

Hadith, the sayings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad, serves as a vital source of guidance for Muslims, providing valuable insights into the interpretation of the Quran and the practical application of Islamic teachings. By studying Hadith, Muslims can gain a deeper understanding of their faith, develop a stronger connection to the Prophet's teachings, and cultivate a more authentic and meaningful spiritual practice.
